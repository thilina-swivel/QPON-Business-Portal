Business Requirements Document (BRD)Corporate Packages Feature Ŵ QPON Platform1. Document Information* Project Name: Corporate Packages Feature* System: QPON Platform* Document Type: Business Requirements Document (BRD)* Version: 1.02. Executive Summary* The Corporate Packages Feature introduces a subscription-based corporate offering to the QPON platform.* This enhancement enables organizations to purchase and manage QPON allocations for employees through structured package tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold) combined with scalable company seat-based subscriptions.3. Business Objectives* Expand QPON services into the corporate market* Increase recurring subscription revenue* Improve employee engagement and savings benefits* Provide companies with measurable ROI through employee savings4. Corporate Package Management* Bronze Package: 10 QPONs for 30 days* Silver Package: 20 QPONs for 30 days* Gold Package: 30 QPONs for 30 days5. Company Tier Management* Starter Package: 20Ŵ200 seats* Growth Package: 200Ŵ1,000 seats* Enterprise Package: 1,000+ seats6. Billing & Subscription Management* Monthly recurring billing* Prorated billing calculations* Credit card payments via Gene/payment gateway* Automatic renewals and failed payment retry handling by payment gateway7. Company Admin Features* Seat allocation and deallocation* Invitation management and resend invitations* Subscription upgrades/downgrades/cancellation* Billing history and invoice access8. Reporting Requirements* Monthly Savings Report* Individual Employee Spending Report8. Deal  RequirementsRequirement Name: Deal Visibility & Corporate Deal ManagementThe system shall support both Global Deals and Corporate Deals with controlled visibility based on user type and company eligibility.Deal TypesDeal TypeDescriptionVisibilityGlobal DealsStandard platform deals available to all usersVisible to all usersCorporate DealsDeals configured for specific companiesVisible only to eligible corporate usersGlobal Deals* Available to all users on the platform * Accessible by: o Individual users o Corporate users * Eligible for: o Deal of the Day o Nearby Deals o General deal listings o Search results Corporate Deals* Restricted to selected companies or corporate groups * Visible only to: o Employees assigned to eligible companies o Users with active corporate subscriptions * Hidden from: o Individual users o Non-eligible companies o Expired or suspended corporate accounts High-Level Flow SummaryCompany Signup      ?Verification      ?Select Tier & Package      ?Configure Seats      ?Payment Setup      ?Subscription Activation      ?Admin Dashboard Access      ?Employee Allocation      ?SMS Invitations      ?Employee Activation      ?Merchant Enablement      ?Monthly Billing & ReportingCompany Onboarding Flow Ŵ Corporate Packages Feature1. Company RegistrationStep 1: Company Admin Signup* Company administrator accesses corporate onboarding portal * Admin enters: o Company name o Business registration details o Contact person information o Email address o Mobile number o Company size o Industry type Step 2: Verification* System validates: o Email address o Mobile number via OTP/SMS * Company account created with ÒPending VerificationÓ status 2. Package & Tier SelectionStep 3: Select Company TierCompany selects appropriate seat tier:TierSeat RangeStarter20Ŵ200Growth200Ŵ1,000Enterprise1,000+Step 4: Select Corporate PackageCompany selects employee package type:PackageQPON AllocationBronze10 QPONsSilver20 QPONsGold30 QPONsStep 5: Configure Seat Count* Admin enters required seat count * System validates against selected tier limits * Monthly pricing calculated dynamically 3. Payment & Subscription ActivationStep 6: Payment Setup* Admin adds corporate credit card * Payment gateway (Stripe/Gene supported gateway) validates payment method Step 7: Subscription CreationSystem creates:* Corporate subscription * Billing cycle * Seat allocation pool * Company profile Step 8: Initial Payment* Initial payment charged * Invoice generated * Subscription status changed to ÒActiveÓ 4. Company Admin Dashboard SetupStep 9: Admin Dashboard AccessCompany admin gains access to:* Seat management * Subscription management * Billing dashboard * Reports dashboard * Employee management 5. Employee OnboardingStep 10: Employee AllocationAdmin:* Allocates seats * Uploads employee details manually or via CSV * Assigns users to available seats Required employee details:* Full name * Mobile number * Email (optional if SMS-only) * Department Step 11: Invitation SendingSystem sends SMS invitation containing:* Activation link * Registration instructions * Corporate package details Step 12: Employee RegistrationEmployee:* Opens invitation link * Creates account or links existing account * Accepts company invitation * Activates corporate package access Step 13: Seat ActivationSystem updates:* Activated seat count * Pending invitations count * Available seat balance 6. Merchant EnablementStep 14: Merchant Corporate ConfigurationMerchant admin:* Enables deals for selected companies * Configures corporate-specific QPON offers System validates:* Company eligibility * Deal availability * QPON allocation rules 7. Ongoing Subscription ManagementSeat Increase Flow1. Admin increases seat count 2. System validates tier limits 3. Prorated amount calculated 4. Immediate payment charged 5. Additional seats activated Seat Decrease Flow1. Admin decreases seat count 2. System validates minimum limits 3. Credit amount calculated 4. Credit applied to next invoice cycle Package Upgrade/Downgrade Flow1. Admin selects new package 2. System recalculates pricing 3. QPON allocations updated 4. Billing adjusted accordingly 8. Renewal & Billing FlowMonthly Renewal* System triggers recurring billing * Payent Invoice generated automatically from payment gateway* QPON allocations refreshed Failed Payment Handling1. Payment retry initiated 2. Notification sent to company admin 3. Grace period applied 4. Subscription suspended if retries fail 9. Reporting & MonitoringCompany admin can access:* Monthly savings report * Employee spending reports * Redemption tracking * Activation summaries 10. Offboarding / Subscription CancellationCompany Cancellation Flow1. Admin submits cancellation request 2. System confirms cancellation 3. Subscription marked for termination at cycle end 4. Users notified via SMS 5. Corporate QPON access removed after expiry
